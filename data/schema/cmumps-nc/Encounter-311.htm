57Regroup Flag Sex (regroup_flag_sex-311) BOOLEANDRG billing requires that a patient have a DRG before the record can be
approved.

After a patient's record has been grouped through the Encoder/Grouper
and a DRG has been assigned to the record, a change to the patient
SEX field (2,.02 and 2.95,9027) requires that the record be grouped again
through the Encoder/Grouper.

If the REGROUP FLAG SEX field is populated with a code of 1, it indicates
that there was a change to the SEX data element which requires the
record to be REGROUPED.

When a DRG has been assigned to a patient's record and a change is
subsequently made to the SEX field, the REGROUP FLAG SEX field is set
to 1. When the inpatient record is finalized in CMUMPS, a check is made
to ensure that the record does not require REGROUPING; if the REGROUP
FLAG SEX is set to 1, the record cannot be finalized (approved) until
it has been grouped again. When the record is grouped again and a DRG
is filed in CMUMPS, the REGROUP FLAG SEX field is cleared (set to NULL).
